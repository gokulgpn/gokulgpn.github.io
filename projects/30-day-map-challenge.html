30 Day Map Challenge 2023 & 2025

A collection of maps produced for the #30DayMapChallenge across 2023 and 2025, exploring different daily themes, tools, datasets and cartographic styles.

About the Challenge

The #30DayMapChallenge is a yearly community mapping event where participants create and share maps on a new theme each day throughout November. It is an opportunity to explore different techniques, tools and datasets — and to communicate spatial stories creatively.
