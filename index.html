About

I am a GIS Analyst based in Munich with experience in spatial data processing, cartography, GTFS analysis, accessibility and mobility studies. I currently work at Plan4Better GmbH, where I support mobility and planning projects using network-based indicators, isochrones, catchments and geospatial data pipelines.

My technical work spans QGIS, PostgreSQL/PostGIS, GDAL/OGR and Python-based geoprocessing (GeoPandas, Pandas, Shapely, Folium), including GTFS feed processing, API-based data collection, location-allocation modelling and reproducible spatial analysis workflows. I am also completing an MSc in Transport Engineering and Mobility at RWTH Aachen University.
